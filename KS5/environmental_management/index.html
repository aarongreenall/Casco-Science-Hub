A-Level Environmental Management

The A-Level Environmental Management course provides a scientific approach to contemporary environmental issues. Students will explore the causes and consequences of human impacts on the environment and develop an understanding of sustainable management strategies. The curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking and practical skills for a wide range of environmental careers.

1. Introduction to Environmental Management 2. Ecosystems and Biodiversity 3. Atmosphere and Climate Change 4. Water Resources and Pollution 5. Land Resources and Agriculture 6. Energy Resources and Sustainability 7. Waste Management and Pollution Control 8. Environmental Law and Policy 9. Environmental Impact Assessment
